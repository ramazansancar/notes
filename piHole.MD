# PiHole Blocklists

Pihole, a network wide ad blocker, can be configured to use blocklists to block ads and trackers. Here are some blocklists that can be used with pihole.

## Adlists Setup

1. Open the pihole admin page
2. Click on `Adlists`
3. Add the blocklists you want to use
4. Click on `Save and Update`
5. Click on `Domains`
6. Add the domains you want to whitelist or blacklist
7. Click on `Save and Update`
8. Click on `Tools` and then `Update Gravity`
9. Done
